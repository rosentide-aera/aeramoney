Common Questions

Is there a budgeting app that works offline?

Yes — Aera works fully offline. You can track expenses, monitor your net worth, and manage budgets without an internet connection. Your data never needs to leave your device.

What is the best free net worth tracker for Android?

Aera is a free Android app that tracks your complete net worth across bank accounts, investments, loans, assets, and debts — with no bank connection required.

Is there a budgeting app that doesn't require linking a bank account?

Aera does not require a bank connection. You enter transactions manually or scan receipts. No bank credentials, no open banking, no data sync with financial institutions.

What is a good free alternative to YNAB or Mint?

Aera is a free personal finance app for Android — no subscription required. It covers budgeting, net worth, investments, goals, and subscriptions in one private app.
